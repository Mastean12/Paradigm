Frequently Asked Questions

How soon will I hear back after submitting my request?

We respond to all consultation requests within 48 hours during business days (Monday-Friday). If you submit on Friday evening or over the weekend, you'll hear from us by Tuesday.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

You can cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before your appointment without charge. Simply contact us by phone or email, and we'll help you find a new time.

Do you offer in-person consultations?

Currently, we only offer virtual consultations via secure video call. This allows us to serve pet owners nationwide and ensures convenience and safety for everyone.

Can I consult about multiple pets?

Yes! For multiple pets, we recommend booking individual consultations for each pet to ensure we can give each one the focused attention they deserve. Contact us for multi-pet package pricing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and Apple Pay. Payment is collected when we confirm your consultation time.

Do you offer follow-up support?

Yes! All consultations include a follow-up email with a summary of recommendations. New Pet Consultation Packages include 30 days of email support for follow-up questions.
