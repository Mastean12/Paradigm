Preparing for Your New Pet

Home Preparation

  • Pet-proof your home
  • Designate sleeping/eating areas
  • Set up safe zones
  • Remove toxic plants/substances

Essential Supplies

  • Appropriate food and water bowls
  • Species-appropriate bedding
  • Toys and enrichment items
  • Grooming supplies
  • Carrier or transport equipment

Health Preparation

  • Schedule vet appointment
  • Research vaccination schedule
  • Find emergency vet nearby
  • Consider pet insurance

First Week Plan

  • Quiet introduction period
  • Establish routine immediately
  • Gradual introduction to family
  • Monitor eating and behavior
